Postgraduate Study

MSc Programme Now Open

MSc in Conservation of Historic Buildings

Department of Architecture & Civil Engineering, University of Bath

Our flagship postgraduate programme trains conservation engineers, architects, and heritage managers to repair, adapt, and manage historic buildings in the 21st century. The MSc combines structural engineering, architectural history, materials science, and heritage policy through studio work, field visits, and a research dissertation.

  • Full-time, one year
  • Practice-led with live conservation projects
  • Taught by active researchers in heritage conservation
  • Graduates work in conservation practices, Historic England, the National Trust, and academia
