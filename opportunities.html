Opportunities

Join our Cluster's collaborative initiatives and contribute to the future of conservation engineering and heritage research.

Get Involved

The History, Conservation & Management Cluster fosters interdisciplinary collaboration among researchers, practitioners, and students. Whether you are looking to engage in a research project, pursue a PhD, or collaborate on new conservation initiatives, we invite you to explore the opportunities below.

PhD Studentship: Regenerative Post-Conflict Heritage Reconstruction

A 2025/26 URSA-funded PhD project focusing on integrating debris re-use, traditional building techniques, and community-centred approaches to rebuild heritage in post-conflict settings.

Open for applications: Spring 2025

Learn more

PhD Studentship: Scaling Up Structural Stone for a Zero-Carbon Future

A multidisciplinary PhD exploring how to scale up demand and production of structural stone in the UK through sustainable, vernacular, and regenerative practices.

Supervisors: Dr. Jiménez Rios, Dr. Hawkins, Prof. Darby

Learn more

PhD Studentship: Recyclability of Earthen Construction Materials

A 2025/26 URSA-funded research project to quantify and assess the recyclability of earthen construction materials to support circular economy practices.

Applications open: Spring 2025

Learn more

Summer Bursary Project: Reeco-Soil

A summer research bursary exploring regenerative retrofitting via ecologically active soil structures, aligning with RENEW's principles of regenerative design and engineering.

Next call: March 2025

Learn more
